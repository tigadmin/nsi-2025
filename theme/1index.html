What is cloud based IT on-demand?

IT-On-Demand refers to a fully managed IT service that connects businesses with a project manager and a pool of skilled IT professionals to complete their projects or tasks. The service offers on-demand access to IT resources, allowing businesses to pay for only what they need when they need it, without having to invest in a full-time IT team.
